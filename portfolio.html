Design A House.

This was our final results as you completed the house designing process. By giving the user two options to customize the house using (yes/no) variables, you can design the color, doors, & windows.


Catching Flowers.

Our game is based off the game "apple catcher". It also catches items falling from the sky with a basket, except this time its falling flowers and not apples. We also made our game minecraft themed. The user has the left right keys to control the movement of the basket, while trying to catch up to 10 flowers then the game will end.
